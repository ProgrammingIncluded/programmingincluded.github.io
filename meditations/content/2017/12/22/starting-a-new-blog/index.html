Meditations on the Word

Purpose: Edification and Gospel Centered


I am starting this section of the site in order to meditate on the Word. Nothing more, nothing less. I was given counsel that I should write down these things as I study and meditate on the Word of God. My goal is not to write about myself or my personal achievements. Rather I would like to focus on WHO God is and HOW God works in my life. I ask for the reader to hold me accountable. Meditations should be glorifying to God. Please let me know if this is not the case and we can discuss about it. I pray that this site will be edifying to the body and engage conversations with those who do not identify as Christians to talk about who Christ is.

This site is kept as a seperate entity from ProgrammingIncluded because ProgrammingIncluded is a site meant as a portfolio. I am still thinking about whether or not it would be wise to include this blog as linked to PI. I need to pray and think more about this.
